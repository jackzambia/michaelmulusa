Film Maker, Speaker & Author

Michael Mulusa is a Filmmaker, Speaker, Entrepreneur and Author. He has featured on a variety of prominent productions such as Zuba, Mpali, as well as Butuku, just to highlight a few. He is the producer of Imizwa – a short film that was selected for the Jordanian international film festivals and London lift off film festival. Michael Mulusa is the writer of Mahali Pa Kuzaliwa, a film about maternal health that will be shot in Kenya this year. The film has won an international grant. Michael has received recognitions(grants) from Africa No – Filter.
